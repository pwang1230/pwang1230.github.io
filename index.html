Research

I'm currently working as a research assistant at Columbia's Computer Graphics and User Interfaces Lab advised by Professor Steven Feiner. More specifically, I work on projects focusing on leveraging VR technologies for virtual task assistance through cues and precues and creating 3D user interfaces that facilitates robot learning.

Prior to this, I've had the privilege to work with Professor Carl Vondrick in the Computer Vision Lab on investigating test time generalization capabilities of neural networks under different novelties and Professor Lydia Chilton in the Computational Design Lab on the abstraction and categorization of visual metaphors.
